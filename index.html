Privacy Policy for Tower to Space

Last updated: April 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tower to Space handles information when you use the game.

1. Information We Collect

Tower to Space itself does not require users to create an account and does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as names, addresses, payment card data, or government identification numbers.

However, the game uses third-party services, including advertising services, which may automatically collect certain technical and device-related information.

2. Advertising

Tower to Space uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and process information such as:

  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising identifiers
  • IP address
  • App interaction and ad performance information
  • Basic device and diagnostic information

This information may be used by Google and its partners to serve ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and improve advertising services.

For more information, please review Google’s policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services

3. How Information Is Used

Information collected through the game and third-party services may be used to:

  • Operate and improve the game
  • Display ads
  • Measure usage and ad performance
  • Detect abuse, fraud, and technical issues

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information directly. However, third-party providers such as Google AdMob may process data according to their own policies and legal obligations.

5. Children’s Privacy

Tower to Space is not specifically directed to children under the age required by applicable local law. If you believe personal data from a child has been provided inappropriately, please contact us and we will review the request.

6. Data Retention

Any technical or advertising-related data processed by third-party services is retained according to those providers’ own policies.

7. Your Choices

Depending on your device, region, and platform settings, you may have options to limit personalized advertising or reset your advertising identifier.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to reduce unnecessary data collection within the game itself. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updated version will be posted on this page with the revised date.
